Description

To make ramen, start by preparing a flavorful broth, which can be based on chicken, pork, miso, or soy sauce depending on the style you prefer. Simmer the broth with aromatics like garlic,ginger, and green onions to deepen the taste. While the broth cooks, boil ramen noddles separately until they are just tender, then drain them. Arrange the noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth over them. Add toppings such as sliced pork(chashu), soft-boiled eggs, nori(seaweed), corn, or bamboo shoots to enhance both flavor and texture. Finally, garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds, and enjoy a comforting bowl of ramen that balances rich broth, chewy noodles, and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • Pork bones(tonkotsu)
  • Vegatable stock
  • Miso paste, soy sauce, or saly for seasoning
  • Fresh or dried ramen noddles(wheat-based, made with kansui for springiness)
  • Chashu(braised pork belly or shoulder)
  • Chicken slices
  • Tofu(for vegetarian ramen)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Corn
  • Bamboo shoots(menma)
  • Spinach or bok choy
  • Mushrooms
  • Soft-boiled eggs(ajitsuke tamago)
  • Nori(seaweed sheets)
  • Green onions(scallions)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Chili paste or oil for spice

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Assemble the bowl
  5. Finish with garnishes
